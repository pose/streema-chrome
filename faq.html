FAQs

  1. What is Streema?
    Streema is an online radio tuner where you can listen to any radio stations that broadcast from around the world.
  2. How do I create an account?
    To do so, go here, it's reaaaally fast and free.
  3. How do I remove/delete a radio station from my Favorites?
    See FAQ #10 on Streema.com.
  4. How do I request a new radio?
    See FAQ #6 on Streema.com
  5. Tech Requirements
    See FAQ#16-21 on Streema.com. NOTE: In case your player is not working, check if it is because of Adobe Flash monopolizing your audio: Press shift+escape and close the "Adobe Flash Plugin" and the "Windows Media Player Plugin". Then try playing again your favorite radios!
