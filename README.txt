Streema is a social networking site where people can listen to, discover and share their favorite AM, FM and Internet radios. Creating a list of favorite radio stations and playing them directly from a web-based platform has never been easier.

Key features:

- Listen and organize your Favorite radios.

- Discover radios through friends and stay tuned with them.

- Search radio stations from more than 230 countries and over 190 genres.

Radio Player for Streema is the the best way to experience Streema in Chrome. Install it and bring radio back into your life!
